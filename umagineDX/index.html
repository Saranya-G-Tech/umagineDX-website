Technology-Driven Solution for Minimizing Food Wastage

UmagineDX Hackathon Project

Problem Statement

Every day, large quantities of edible food are wasted by restaurants, events, and households, while many people suffer from hunger. The lack of a direct communication platform between donors and receivers results in inefficient food redistribution.

Proposed Solution

Our solution is a technology-driven platform that directly connects food donors with NGOs and needy individuals. Donors can post food availability in real time, and nearby receivers can respond immediately, reducing food wastage effectively.

Technologies Used

  • HTML & CSS for web development
  • Responsive web design
  • Location-based matching system
  • Real-time notification mechanism

Impact & Benefits

  • Significant reduction in food wastage
  • Faster and efficient food distribution
  • Support for NGOs and underprivileged communities
  • Scalable and socially impactful solution
